From signal to accountable draft.

WireTrove is designed to support editorial judgment while preserving the chain of evidence behind it.

Step 1

Collect

Gather reporting only from explicitly managed sources.

Step 2

Compare

Group related coverage and distinguish independent publishers.

Step 3

Prepare

Create a structured draft with facts, uncertainty, risk, and source context.

Step 4

Review

A human editor edits, holds, rejects, or approves the exact version.

“Automation should make editorial judgment better informed—not optional.”

WireTrove remains an internal prototype. It does not autonomously publish, create social accounts, or bypass human approval.
